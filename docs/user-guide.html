Overview

What is the Whole Life Challenge?

The Whole Life Challenge is a 75-day programme where you build sustainable habits across 7 areas of your life. Each day you score points for completing habits and maintaining good nutrition. The app tracks your progress, calculates bonuses for consistency, provides AI-powered reflexion prompts, and lets you compete on a real-time leaderboard.

The 7 Pillars

The habits you track every day

Each day has a nutrition score plus 6 trackable habits. Together they form the 7 pillars of the challenge.

Nutrition (0-5 pts)

Start with 5 points and deduct 1 for each non-compliant food choice. Linked to the WLC Nutrition Levels Guide.

Foundation

Exercise (5 pts)

10+ minutes of activity. Select your type from a dropdown (HIIT, Running, Weights, etc.) and log duration.

5 Points

Mobilise (5 pts)

10 minutes of stretching, foam rolling, yoga, or similar mobility work. Log type and duration.

5 Points

Sleep (5 pts)

Log your sleep hours using preset buttons (5h to 10.5h in half-hour increments).

5 Points

Hydrate (5 pts)

Track water intake with tap-to-add buttons. Visual progress bar fills toward your daily target (default 2000ml).

5 Points

Well-Being (5 pts)

Complete a weekly well-being practice. Describe your activity in a free-form text entry.

5 Points

Reflect (5 pts)

Write a daily journal entry. AI-powered prompts are available to inspire your reflexion.

5 Points

Daily Scoring

How your points add up

Your daily score is the sum of your nutrition score plus 5 points for each completed habit. A perfect day is 35 points.

ComponentPointsHow It Works
Nutrition0 – 5Start at 5, deduct 1 per non-compliant food choice
Exercise0 or 55 points when 10+ min activity logged
Mobilise0 or 55 points when 10+ min mobility logged
Sleep0 or 55 points when sleep hours recorded
Hydrate0 or 55 points when daily water target reached
Well-Being0 or 55 points when practice described
Reflect0 or 55 points when reflexion written
Total0 – 35Perfect day = 35 points
ⓘ The Stats Row at the top of Check In shows your daily score, cumulative total, completion rate, and current streak of perfect (35/35) days.

Bonus System

Rewards for consistency

Four bonus types reward sustained effort. Bonuses are earned through streaks and auto-apply when you miss a habit (except Free Day, which is manual).

Indulgence

Earn: 18+ nutrition points over 4 consecutive days.
Effect: Auto-restores nutrition to 5/5 on a bad food day.

Auto-Apply

Rest Day

Earn: 10 consecutive days of completed exercise.
Effect: Auto-covers a missed exercise day.

Auto-Apply

Night Owl

Earn: 6 consecutive days of completed sleep logging.
Effect: Auto-covers a missed sleep day.

Auto-Apply

Free Day

Earn: 21 consecutive days with a score of 34 or higher.
Effect: Marks the entire day as 35/35. Must be manually activated.

Manual
ⓘ The Bonus Tracker at the bottom of Check In shows your earned/available/used count for each bonus, plus a progress bar toward the next one.

Journal

Review your reflections

The Journal page lists all your reflections in reverse chronological order (newest first). Each entry shows the day number, date, full reflexion text, and that day's score out of 35. Reflections are written via the Reflect modal on Check In — the Journal is read-only.

ⓘ Reflections are always private — even when you opt in to the leaderboard, your journal entries are never shared with other users.
🏅

Leaderboard

Compete with other challengers

The Board page shows a ranked list of all users who have opted in. Participation is entirely optional.

  • 🔓 Opt in — Toggle "Join the leaderboard" on the Board page. Off by default.
  • 🏆 Rankings — Sorted by total score. Top 3 get medal icons. Your entry is highlighted with a "You" badge.
  • 📈 Stats shown — Display name, days active, current streak, and total score.
  • ⚡ Real-time updates — The leaderboard auto-refreshes when anyone's data changes via Supabase subscriptions.
  • 📊 Progress comparison — Opted-in competitors appear as overlay lines on the Cumulative Score chart on the Progress page.
⚠ Only your display name and scores are visible to others. Reflections, recovery data, and other personal metrics remain private.
